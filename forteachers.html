Welcome to the Museum!

The museum is open for children of all ages, and we provide tours and activities for the whole class!

We also offer activities in the Maker Space at our museum, as well as sending our Senior Makers to the class for a Maker Day. Read more below!

Bring the class to the museum!

Join a guided tour of the museum and our exhibits! Sign your class up for a tour by sending an email to education@myscimuseum.com or click the button below to read more about or exhibits.

Our exhibits

Bring the museum to class!

If it's not possible to bring the class to the museum, you can bring the museum to the class! Our Senior Makers are happy to take a trip to your school, where they engage the class through practically applied science.

Send us an email at education@myscimuseum.com and we'll find the activities that are right for your class!

Coding for the class

We also offer programming lessons for your class, which are available for kids 8-16. The classes range from basic logical thinking to writing your own websites and games.

We can send out a Senior Maker to your school provided you have computers for your class, or you can bring your class to us. Sign up via email to education@myscimuseum.com!
