Welcome to the Maker Space!

Welcome to our maker space! Here we explore, experiment and learn via practical application of science. You can build anything from websites to gravity racers and your own circut boards!

The Maker Club is open to kids of all ages and you can sign up by sending an email to signup@makersclub.com!

Code Camp

Become a code champ with our Code Camp!

The camp is open to anyone interested in learning about programming through a mix of practice and open classroom teaching. The camp begins the 5th of May, and takes place every thursday until the 30th of June.

Sign up by sending an email to signup@makersclub.com!

Maker Days

Are you curious about the Maker Space, but unsure about joining the Makers Club? Don't fret, you can always join our Maker Days.

The Maker Days are open to anyone and you don't need to sign up beforehand. Everyone is welcome and we have projects for all ages 😊 We have Maker Days every other saturday, marked yellow in the Event Calendar!
