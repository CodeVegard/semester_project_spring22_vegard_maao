Explore the Exhibits!

Welcome to My Sci Museum! This is a space where we explore and learn together. Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

Ant Colonies and Societies

Our exhibits are made to inspire future scientists and researchers to explore and investigate!

Learn about the secret life of ants, and how one of the smallest creatures have gotten one of the biggest populations on earth! This exhibit is open from April to August.

Ice Age Antics

While this Mammoth might not have much wool left, it's still a amazing sight to see! Explore our Pleistocene exhibit and learn about the amazing and strange creatures of the Ice Age! Open all year.

Bring the class to the museum!

Join a guided tour of the museum and our exhibits! Sign your class up for a tour by sending an email to education@myscimuseum.com. You can click here for more information for schools and teachers!
