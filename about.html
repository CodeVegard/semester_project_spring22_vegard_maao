About us

My Sci Museum has been open since 1994. In our 28 years of staying open, we have done our very best to be an institution for learning, exploration and fun for children of all ages!

Admission

Entry to My Sci Museum is free! You can come as you please to enjoy our exhibits and explore the museum by yourself.

All we want is for you to enjoy yourself and ask our staff any questions you might have 😊

Tours

We also have guided tours of the museum. The tours cost 2,5 dollars per person, or 25 dollars for groups of 10 or more people. Tours can be booked at our reception desk, or via our email booking@myscimuseum.com.
